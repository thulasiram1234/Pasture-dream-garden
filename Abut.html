    Pasture Landscaping

    Est.1997

    Local, Quality Services

    We provide quality landscape installation and maintenance for residential, commercial and
    strata properties in the Mainland area. Our goal is to create natural, environmentally-friendly
    landscapes that are easy to care for. With decades of experience, Pasture Landscaping offers a
    wide range of services within the landscaping industries, including landscape construction,
    grounds maintenance, irrigation installation and maintenance.

    We are a friendly company that will recreate or bring your landscaping back to life. Pasture
    Landscaping is dedicated to providing one on one customer service which includes a free
    consultation to ensure you get exactly what you are looking for.
